Chester Bennington

A legendary vocalist who touched generations with his powerful and emotional voice.

Theme

Here's a time line of Chester's life:

  • 1976 – Born in Phoenix, Arizona, USA.
  • 1993 – Begins his musical career with the band Grey Daze.
  • 1999 – Joins Linkin Park after a successful audition.
  • 2000 – Releases Hybrid Theory, which becomes a worldwide success.
  • 2003 – Releases Meteora, solidifying the band’s global fame.
  • 2005 – Starts the side project Dead by Sunrise.
  • 2007 – Linkin Park releases Minutes to Midnight, showing a more mature sound.
  • 2013 – Becomes the lead singer of Stone Temple Pilots for a period.
  • 2017 – Releases One More Light, his final album with Linkin Park.
  • July 20, 2017 – Chester tragically passes away at age 41.

Legacy and Mental Health Awareness

Chester often spoke openly about his struggles with depression and addiction, inspiring millions to seek help and to talk about mental health without fear or shame.
